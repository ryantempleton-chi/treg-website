about ryan

Ryan Templeton is the Founder & Lead Broker of Templeton Real Estate Group — a boutique, Chicago-based team built around clarity, responsiveness, and long-term client relationships.

With nearly a decade of experience across rentals, purchases, and sales, Ryan is known for organized communication, thoughtful negotiation, and a calm approach — even in competitive or fast-moving situations.

Whether you’re renting your first apartment, buying your next home, or planning a move months in advance, Ryan’s focus stays the same: keep the process clear, proactive, and personal.
