about

templeton real estate group.

A Chicago-based boutique real estate team helping clients buy, sell, and rent across the city and suburbs — with modern marketing, clear communication, and service that feels personal from start to finish.

built on relationships, not transactions

The Templeton Real Estate Group was created with a simple idea: combine the reach and resources of a major brokerage with the care, creativity, and follow-through of a boutique team.

Our clients come to us at every stage of life — renting their first Chicago apartment, buying a condo or single-family home, upsizing, downsizing, or relocating to a new neighborhood or suburb. No matter the price point or property type, the goal is the same: make the process feel clear, calm, and intentional.

We lead with education and expectations, so you always know what’s happening, what comes next, and what your options are. From pricing strategies and neighborhood comparisons to timing and offer structure, the Templeton Real Estate Group is here to help you buy, sell, and rent with confidence — not confusion.

meet ryan, your lead broker

Ryan Templeton is the founder and lead broker behind the Templeton Real Estate Group.

With nearly a decade of experience helping Chicagoans buy, sell, and rent, Ryan has built a relationship-driven business grounded in clarity, strategy, and genuine care. He’s known for quick communication, honest guidance, and a calm, organized approach — whether he’s coordinating dozens of rental showings or navigating a complex purchase.

Ryan started the Templeton Real Estate Group after years of steady growth and hundreds of successful transactions. The vision was simple: create a team where clients feel known, heard, and supported long after the closing table — a team that clients come back to for their next move, and the one after that.

When he’s not working with clients, you’ll likely find Ryan exploring new corners of the city, trying out Chicago’s latest restaurants, or soaking up the energy of local festivals, sporting events, and seasonal happenings.

a growing chicago real estate team

As demand for clear, straightforward guidance has grown, so has the Templeton Real Estate Group. The team is intentionally built — adding people who share the same values around communication, follow-through, and treating every client like a long-term relationship.

Each team member will have their own focus area — from rentals and first-time buyers to move-up sales and suburban moves — while staying aligned on one shared mission: to be your trusted real estate resource in every season of life.
