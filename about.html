ryan templeton.

founder & lead broker, the templeton real estate group · keller williams ONEchicago

When it comes to real estate, Ryan believes it's never just about the property — it’s about people.

With nearly a decade of experience helping Chicagoans buy, sell, and rent across the city, Ryan has built a reputation for delivering clarity, strategy, and genuine care in every transaction. His approach blends modern marketing, deep neighborhood knowledge, and an easygoing professionalism that puts clients instantly at ease.

After years of steady growth — and hundreds of successful rentals, purchases, and sales — Ryan created the Templeton Real Estate Group with a simple vision: combine the reach and resources of a major brokerage with the personalized service and creativity of a boutique firm.

Today, he leads a growing team dedicated to that mission: helping clients navigate Chicago’s neighborhoods with confidence, while feeling supported every step of the way.

More than real estate

Chicago isn’t just where Ryan works — it’s home, and the heartbeat behind everything he does.

Whether he's exploring new neighborhoods, checking out one of Chicago’s newest restaurants, or soaking up the city’s energy at festivals, sporting events, and seasonal happenings, Ryan draws inspiration from the people and places that make the city special.

That same curiosity shapes his approach with clients: ask questions, understand their priorities, and guide them with transparency and care — always with a long-term relationship in mind.

the templeton real estate group

What started as a one-person business has grown into a team built on shared values: genuine service, honest guidance, and treating every client as a long-term relationship.

As the Templeton Real Estate Group expands, each team member brings their own strengths — from rentals and first-time buyers to move-up purchases and suburban sales — all united under the same commitment to communication, clarity, and results.
