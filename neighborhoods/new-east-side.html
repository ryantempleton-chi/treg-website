Life in the New East Side

Known for its clean, master-planned design, the New East Side offers a peaceful residential feel while sitting right next to downtown.

Parks, dog-friendly green spaces, and wide walkways create a calm environment — especially compared to surrounding neighborhoods. Residents enjoy being steps from Millennium Park, Maggie Daley Park, the lakefront trail, and the Riverwalk.

Grocery stores, cafés, and everyday conveniences are built into the community, making it easy to run errands or enjoy the outdoors without traveling far.

Who this area works well for

  • Professionals who want downtown access without downtown noise
  • Renters drawn to full-amenity living in newer high-rises
  • Buyers who value convenience, walkability, and lakefront proximity

Homes & rentals in the New East Side

The New East Side offers primarily high-rise living — with luxury rental towers and full-service condo buildings dominating the area.

What you’ll typically see

  • Newer high-rise rentals with top-tier amenities
  • Condo buildings with pools, gyms, and concierge services
  • Units with lake, river, or skyline views
