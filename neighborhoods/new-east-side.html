new east side.

New East Side is a modern pocket of downtown Chicago — a cluster of high-rises wrapped around parks, the lakefront, and the Riverwalk, with quiet residential streets just steps from the Loop.

Life in New East Side

New East Side feels like a private pocket of downtown — calm, walkable, and surrounded by water, parks, and skyline views.

Tucked between Michigan Avenue, the lakefront, and the river, New East Side offers a unique blend of green space and high-rise living. Residents have easy access to Maggie Daley Park, Millennium Park, the Riverwalk, and the lakefront path, all within a few minutes' walk.

Even though it’s surrounded by downtown activity, the neighborhood itself can feel surprisingly quiet and residential, with internal park spaces, dog-friendly paths, and building-to-building connectivity that make it easy to live, work, and relax in a small radius.

Who this area works well for

  • Professionals who want to walk to the Loop or nearby offices
  • Residents who prioritize views, amenities, and modern buildings
  • Anyone who wants downtown convenience with more green space than a typical core business district

Homes & rentals in New East Side

New East Side is primarily high-rise living — with a strong mix of luxury rentals and condo buildings, many offering extensive amenity packages and impressive views.

What you’ll typically see

  • Modern rental towers with full-service amenities
  • Condo buildings with lake, park, or skyline views
  • Larger floor plans and higher-floor residences geared toward long-term downtown living
