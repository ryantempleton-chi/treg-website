roscoe village.

Roscoe Village is a charming, neighborhood-focused pocket of the city — known for its “village” main street, family-friendly vibe, and relaxed, local feel.

Charming · laid-back · family-friendly

Walk-ups · condos · single-family homes · small multi-unit buildings

Transit access
Brown Line nearby · buses · easy access to major north side corridors

Life in Roscoe Village

Roscoe Village feels like a small town tucked inside the city — with a walkable main street and quiet residential blocks surrounding it.

Along Roscoe Street, you’ll find coffee shops, boutiques, casual restaurants, ice cream spots, and neighborhood bars that stay busy without feeling overwhelming. Seasonal events and street festivals help reinforce the “village” feel and keep things community-oriented.

Just a block or two away, residential streets are lined with walk-ups, condos, and single-family homes, many with front yards, porches, and a quieter energy than some of the busier north side neighborhoods. It’s a great fit for anyone who wants to feel rooted in a true community.

Who this area works well for

  • Renters who want a calm, walkable neighborhood with local staples
  • Buyers seeking condos or single-family homes on quiet streets
  • Residents who value a “neighborhood within the city” feel, with easy access to other north side areas

Homes & rentals in Roscoe Village

Roscoe Village offers a comfortable mix of rental options and ownership opportunities, with many properties updated while still retaining classic Chicago charm.

What you’ll typically see

  • Vintage walk-ups and courtyard-style rentals
  • Condo conversions and smaller multi-unit buildings
  • Single-family homes and newer construction on wider, residential blocks
