Avondale is a Northwest Side pocket with classic Chicago flats, tree-lined streets, and a growing mix of restaurants, bars, and cafés — close to Logan Square, but a bit more laid-back.

Life in Avondale

Avondale feels like classic Chicago — calm residential streets, corner staples, and a growing lineup of local spots that make it easy to stay close to home.

You’ll see a mix of long-time residents and newer arrivals, many choosing Avondale for its balance of value, space, and proximity to neighborhoods like Logan Square, Roscoe Village, and Irving Park. Main corridors bring more restaurants, bars, and cafés each year, while quieter side streets offer a comfortable, everyday pace.

Whether you’re looking for a classic two-flat, a walk-up condo, or more room to grow, Avondale offers a “just-right” mix of neighborhood calm with easy access to the city.

Where you’ll find Avondale
Northwest Side · along the Kennedy Expressway and the Blue Line
Transit: Blue Line · I-90/94 · multiple CTA bus routes
Nearby: Logan Square · Roscoe Village · Irving Park
