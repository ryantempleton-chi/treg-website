Life in Avondale

Avondale feels like classic Chicago — calm residential streets, corner staples, and a growing lineup of spots that make it easy to stay local.

You'll see a mix of long-time residents and newer arrivals, with many choosing Avondale for its balance of value, space, and access to nearby neighborhoods like Logan Square, Roscoe Village, and Irving Park. Main corridors bring in more restaurants, bars, and cafés each year, while quieter side streets offer a comfortable, everyday pace.

Neighborhood Highlights

A few spots that help define the Avondale experience.

Revolution Brewing — Taproom

One of Chicago’s best-known breweries, offering guided tours, tastings, and a lively industrial-chic taproom experience. A local staple for beer lovers.

Avondale Bowl

A vintage 8-lane bowling alley restored with incredible character — cocktails, small bites, and a perfect retro vibe.

First Ascent Climbing Gym

A popular bouldering and fitness hub offering classes, community events, and one of the best climbing facilities on the Northwest Side.
