Life in the Gold Coast

The Gold Coast blends historic charm with lakefront access — offering a calmer, more residential feel just steps from some of the city’s busiest districts.

Stately homes, tree-lined streets, and classic buildings give the neighborhood a timeless character. Residents enjoy quick access to the Lakefront Trail, Oak Street Beach, and nearby shopping and dining along Rush Street, Oak Street, and Michigan Avenue.

Despite its proximity to major commercial areas, many blocks in the Gold Coast feel quiet and residential, with a strong sense of history and architecture that sets it apart from other downtown neighborhoods.

Who this area works well for

  • Residents who want a refined, residential feel close to downtown
  • Buyers seeking condos, co-ops, or architecturally significant homes
  • Renters who want lakefront access and walkability in a calmer setting

Homes & rentals in the Gold Coast

The Gold Coast offers a range of high-end options — from historic co-ops and classic walk-ups to full-amenity high-rises with lake and skyline views.

What you’ll typically see

  • Luxury high-rises and condo buildings near the lake
  • Vintage walk-up apartments on tree-lined side streets
  • Historic single-family homes and rowhouses on select blocks
