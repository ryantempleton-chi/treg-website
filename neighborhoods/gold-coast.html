Chicago neighborhood guide

gold coast.

The Gold Coast is one of Chicago’s most established lakefront neighborhoods — a mix of historic mansions, elegant walk-ups, and classic high-rises just north of downtown.

Life in the Gold Coast

Living in the Gold Coast means tree-lined streets, vintage architecture, and easy access to both the lake and the city’s top shopping and dining districts.

Lakefront access

Oak Street Beach and the Lakefront Trail are right next door — an easy routine for walks, runs, and skyline views.

Homes & architecture

Historic mansions and walk-ups sit alongside classic high-rises, co-ops, and full-amenity towers — many with lake or skyline views.

Dining & shopping

Steps from Oak Street and the Mag Mile, with Rush Street and nearby neighborhoods offering everything from quiet cafés to destination dining.

Neighborhood snapshot

Where you’ll find the Gold Coast
Near North Side · along the lakefront just north of downtown
Local orientation
  • Nearby: Old Town · River North · Streeterville
  • Boundaries: Roughly between North Ave and Chicago Ave, from the lake to Clark St
  • Good to know: The Gold Coast is highly walkable — you’ll feel the difference block-by-block between quiet residential streets and busy commercial corridors.
