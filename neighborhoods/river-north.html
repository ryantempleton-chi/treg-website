Life in River North

River North brings together restaurants, nightlife, shopping, and convenience — all within walking distance of the Loop and the Riverwalk.

Residents enjoy an almost unmatched level of access — from dining and bars to grocery stores, fitness studios, coffee shops, and cultural attractions. The neighborhood stays active day and night.

Side streets vary widely: some are busier and close to nightlife, while others offer a quieter, residential feel with modern mid-rise and high-rise buildings.

Who this area works well for

  • Renters and buyers who want a downtown lifestyle
  • Residents who prioritize walkability and access to amenities
  • Buyers looking for condos, lofts, or newer high-rise living

Homes & rentals in River North

River North offers some of Chicago’s best high-rise options — with modern luxury rentals, amenity-packed towers, and contemporary condo buildings.

What you’ll typically see

  • Luxury high-rise rentals with top amenities
  • Modern condo buildings with skyline and river views
  • Loft conversions with exposed brick and timber
