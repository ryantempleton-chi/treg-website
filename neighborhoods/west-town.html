west town.

West Town is a collection of neighborhoods — including Ukrainian Village, East Village, and Noble Square — known for classic flats, creative energy, and easy access to downtown.

Life in West Town

West Town feels residential and creative at the same time — a patchwork of distinct pockets with restaurants, bars, and quiet streets just blocks apart.

Housing is dominated by vintage two- and three-flats, worker cottages, greystones, and newer condo developments. Commercial corridors along Chicago, Division, and Western continue to grow with local favorites.

With Blue Line access and quick routes into the West Loop and downtown, West Town is frequently cross-shopped with Wicker Park, Logan Square, and River West.
