Chicago neighborhood guide

west town.

West Town covers a collection of neighborhoods — including Ukrainian Village, East Village, Noble Square, and parts of Humboldt Park — with classic flats, tree-lined streets, and growing restaurant and bar corridors.

Life in West Town

West Town feels like a patchwork of smaller neighborhoods, each with its own character — but all sharing a laid-back, residential feel close to the city’s core.

Neighborhood pockets

Ukrainian Village, East Village, and Noble Square each have their own vibe — with classic storefront corridors and quieter blocks just a street or two away.

Homes & feel

A mix of vintage two- and three-flats, worker cottages, greystones, and newer condos — with an “everyday Chicago” neighborhood rhythm.

Commute & access

Blue Line access plus bus routes along Chicago, Division, and Western — and quick I-90/94 connectivity for downtown, the West Loop, and beyond.

Neighborhood snapshot

Where you’ll find West Town
Northwest of downtown · between the Kennedy Expressway and Humboldt Park
Local orientation
  • Nearby: Wicker Park · Humboldt Park · Logan Square · Ukrainian Village
  • Boundaries: Varies by pocket — generally west of downtown and east of Humboldt Park
  • Good to know: “West Town” is a larger umbrella that includes multiple micro-neighborhoods, so listings can feel very different block-to-block.
  • Tip: If you’re comparing neighborhoods, West Town is often cross-shopped with Wicker Park, Logan Square, and River West.

Neighborhood highlights

A few local favorites that give West Town its character. These are great starting points if you’re getting to know the neighborhood cluster.

ukrainian village.

historic streets · churches · cafes

A landmarked pocket of West Town with tree-lined streets, brick walk-ups, ornate churches, and a long-running mix of bakeries, cafés, and bars.

Centered around W Chicago Ave & N Damen Ave, Chicago, IL 60622

chicago avenue corridor.

restaurants · bars · coffee shops

A growing stretch of restaurants, neighborhood bars, galleries, and coffee shops that runs through the heart of West Town.

W Chicago Ave between Milwaukee & Western, Chicago, IL 60622

humboldt park.

park · lagoon · trails

A large historic park with a lagoon, boathouse, trails, sports fields, and cultural events, just west of many West Town blocks.

1400 N Humboldt Dr, Chicago, IL 60622
visit website

the 606 / bloomingdale trail.

elevated trail · biking · running

An elevated greenway and trail that runs along the northern edge of West Town, connecting to Logan Square, Bucktown, and Wicker Park.

Access points along W Bloomingdale Ave near N Western & N California

morton salt complex.

industrial icon · riverfront

The historic Morton Salt complex along the river — once a working facility, now a recognizable industrial landmark and part of a redeveloped riverfront.

1357 N Elston Ave, Chicago, IL 60642

the salt shed.

music venue · riverfront · events

A newer indoor/outdoor music and events venue along the river that makes creative use of the former Morton Salt site.

1357 N Elston Ave, Chicago, IL 60642
