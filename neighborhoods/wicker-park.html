Life in Wicker Park

Wicker Park is one of Chicago’s most recognizable neighborhoods — walkable, lively, and packed with independent shops, coffee spots, and dining. The neighborhood centers around Milwaukee / Damen / North, with a strong “grab dinner, browse a store, catch a show” rhythm that runs day and night. Housing ranges from classic flats and walk-ups to newer condos and modern builds mixed in throughout.

Shops & dining

Milwaukee, Damen, and North are lined with independent boutiques, neighborhood favorites, and a deep bench of restaurants and cocktail bars.

Homes & feel

A blend of vintage walk-ups, classic Chicago flats, brick two- and three-flats, plus a strong condo market — with newer construction sprinkled in.

Commute & access

The Blue Line (Damen / Division) anchors the neighborhood, with strong bus coverage and quick access to downtown, Logan Square, and O’Hare.
