Chicago neighborhood guide
            

lake view.

            

              Lake View blends classic Chicago flats, lakefront access, and some of the city’s most               famous entertainment districts — from Wrigley Field to the bars and restaurants along               Broadway, Halsted, and Southport.             

          
                     
            

Life in Lake View

            

              Lake View feels like a big neighborhood made up of smaller pockets — Wrigleyville,               Boystown, the lakefront, and quieter side streets in between.             

            

              On game days, streets around Wrigley Field are packed with Cubs fans and pre-game               patios. Along Broadway and Halsted, you’ll find LGBTQ+ bars and nightspots, cafés,               and long-time neighborhood institutions. Farther west, the Southport Corridor adds a               slightly calmer, family-friendly stretch of boutiques, restaurants, and walkable               blocks.             

            

              Lakefront paths, beaches, Belmont Harbor, and the golf course give residents easy               access to green space in warmer months — while Red, Brown, and Purple Line trains make               it simple to commute downtown or to other North Side neighborhoods.

Neighborhood highlights

            

              A few local favorites that give Lake View its character. These are great starting               points if you’re getting to know the neighborhood.             

          
          
                                                   
              
                                 
                  
                                       
                  
                    

wrigley field.

                    
ballpark · concerts · game day
                    

                      Home of the Chicago Cubs and one of baseball’s most storied parks, surrounded                       by bars, patios, and classic game-day energy.                     

                    
                      1060 W Addison St, Chicago, IL 60613                     
                  
                
                                 
                  
                                       
                  
                    

gallagher way.

                    
plaza · events · family-friendly
                    

                      A central gathering space with outdoor movies, markets, ice skating, and                       family events right next to the ballpark.                     

                    
                      3635 N Clark St, Chicago, IL 60613                     
                  
                
                                 
                  
                                       
                  
                    

belmont harbor & lakefront.

                    
harbor · green space · lakefront trail
                    

                      A popular marina and park area with walking paths, dog-friendly spots, and                       direct access to the Lakefront Trail.                     

                    
                      3200 N Lake Shore Dr, Chicago, IL 60657                     
                  
                
                                 
                  
                                       
                  
                    

sydney r. marovitz golf course.

                    
9-hole course · lake views
                    

                      A scenic 9-hole course along the lakefront with skyline views and a                       neighborhood feel.                     

                    
                      3600 N Recreation Dr, Chicago, IL 60613                     
                  
                
                                 
                  
                                       
                  
                    

tbox bar crawl.

                    
annual event · nightlife · costumes
                    

                      A well-known holiday season bar crawl centered around Lake View’s taverns and                       pubs — loud, festive, and very neighborhood-specific.
