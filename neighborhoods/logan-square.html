logan square.

Centered around its historic boulevards and busy Milwaukee Avenue corridor, Logan Square mixes classic Chicago flats with a strong restaurant, bar, and music scene — all anchored by the Blue Line.

Life in Logan Square

Logan Square has a creative, neighborhood feel — leafy boulevards, historic greystones, and a constant flow of new restaurants, bars, and cafés.

Milwaukee, Kedzie, and Logan Boulevard form the core of the neighborhood, with cocktail bars, breweries, coffee shops, and venues clustered around the Blue Line station. Just off the main corridors, side streets feel quieter and more residential with classic two- and three-flats, courtyard buildings, and updated single-family homes.

It’s a popular choice for renters and buyers who want a lively, walkable area with easy transit to downtown, strong neighborhood identity, and plenty of options for dining out, grabbing a drink, or catching a show close to home.
