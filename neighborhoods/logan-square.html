Life in Logan Square

Logan Square is known for its mix of historic architecture, thriving food scene, and community feel — making it one of Chicago’s most sought-after neighborhoods.

The main boulevards and Milwaukee Avenue offer countless restaurants, breweries, cocktail bars, and cafés. The weekly Logan Square Farmers Market draws huge crowds, and local businesses define much of the neighborhood’s identity.

Step onto residential streets and things quiet down quickly. You’ll find greystones, vintage three-flats, and newer developments that maintain the neighborhood’s architectural variety and charm.

Who this area works well for

  • Renters who want nightlife, culture, and dining at their doorstep
  • Buyers drawn to character-filled homes with Chicago charm
  • Residents who enjoy a creative, community-driven atmosphere

Homes & rentals in Logan Square

Logan Square’s housing options are diverse, offering something for every stage of life and budget.

What you’ll typically see
