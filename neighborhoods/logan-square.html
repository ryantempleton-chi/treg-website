Chicago neighborhood guide

logan square.

Centered around its historic boulevards and busy Milwaukee Avenue corridor, Logan Square mixes classic Chicago flats with a strong restaurant, bar, and music scene — all anchored by the Blue Line.

Life in Logan Square

Logan Square has a creative, neighborhood feel — leafy boulevards, historic greystones, and a constant flow of new restaurants, bars, and cafés.

Food & nightlife

Milwaukee Avenue is the spine — cocktail bars, breweries, coffee shops, and late-night spots clustered near the Logan Square Blue Line station.

Homes & feel

Classic two- and three-flats, greystones, courtyard buildings, and updated single-family homes — especially on the quieter side streets off the main corridors.

Commute & access

Blue Line access keeps downtown commutes simple, with I-90/94 nearby and strong biking along the Milwaukee Avenue corridor.
