Life in Logan Square

Logan Square blends historic charm with a growing lineup of restaurants, bars, and local businesses — creating a neighborhood that feels both lived-in and continually evolving.

The Logan Boulevard and Kedzie Boulevard corridors showcase some of the neighborhood’s most iconic architecture and green spaces. Along Milwaukee Avenue and other main streets, you’ll find coffee shops, cocktail bars, breweries, and restaurants that stay busy day and night.

Side streets tend to be quieter and more residential, with classic Chicago two-flats, multi-units, and single-family homes offering a range of options for renters and buyers.

Who this area works well for

  • Renters who want a mix of neighborhood feel and nightlife
  • Buyers considering condos, multi-units, or single-family homes
  • Residents who value character, local businesses, and convenient access to the Blue Line and downtown

Homes & rentals in Logan Square

Logan Square offers a range of housing options — from vintage flats and courtyard buildings to newer condos and renovated single-family homes.

What you’ll typically see

  • Vintage walk-ups and two- and three-flats
  • Condo conversions and newer mid-sized buildings
  • Single-family homes and multi-units on tree-lined residential streets
