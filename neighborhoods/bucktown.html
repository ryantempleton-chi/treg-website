Neighborhood Highlights

A few spots that give Bucktown its mix of neighborhood comfort, local nightlife, and easy access to the rest of the city.

the 606 / bloomingdale trail.

An elevated park and path running through Bucktown and neighboring areas — perfect for walking, jogging, biking, or just getting a new perspective on the neighborhood.

elevated trail · parks · neighborhood views

diversey rock n' bowl.

Classic bowling alley energy with music, leagues, and late-night hangs — a fun option for group events and casual nights out.

bowling · leagues · nightlife

lottie's pub.

A neighborhood staple with sports on TV, comfort food, and a laid-back crowd — the kind of spot people return to again and again.

neighborhood bar · game day · patio

midtown athletic club.

A full-service fitness and racquet club offering tennis, swimming, classes, and wellness amenities — a major draw for active residents.

fitness · tennis · wellness

whirlyball.

A one-of-a-kind spot combining bumper cars, lacrosse-style scoops, and a basketball hoop — plus bowling, events, and a big social atmosphere.

group events · activities · nightlife
