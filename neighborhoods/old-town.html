Life in Old Town

Old Town offers charm and character — with historic architecture, boutiques, restaurants, comedy clubs, and tree-lined residential pockets that feel distinctly Chicago.

Wells Street acts as the main commercial corridor, filled with restaurants, bars, shops, and outdoor patios that stay active throughout the year. Just a few steps off the main roads, quieter streets showcase vintage buildings, classic walk-ups, and thoughtfully restored homes.

Residents appreciate Old Town’s access to nearby neighborhoods like Lincoln Park, Gold Coast, and River North — while still enjoying a strong sense of community and local character.

Who this area works well for

  • Renters who want charm, walkability, and nightlife
  • Buyers looking for condos, townhomes, or single-family homes with character
  • Residents who enjoy boutique shopping, dining, comedy clubs, and a lively neighborhood feel

Homes & rentals in Old Town

Old Town offers a wide range of housing — from charming vintage units to modern condo buildings and renovated single-family homes on quieter residential streets.

What you’ll typically see

  • Vintage walk-ups with classic layouts
  • Condo conversions and modern mid-rises
  • Townhomes and single-family homes with historic details
