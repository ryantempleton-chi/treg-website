Life in Old Town

Old Town is one of Chicago’s most recognized neighborhoods — a blend of vintage architecture, vibrant energy, and walkable streets.

Wells Street is the heart of the neighborhood, lined with restaurants, patios, bars, boutiques, coffee shops, and local businesses. Its walkability is one of its biggest draws, especially for residents who want everything close by.

Just a few blocks off Wells, the neighborhood becomes quieter and more residential. Tree-lined streets, historic homes, and small parks provide a balance that attracts both long-term homeowners and next-step renters.

Who this area works well for

  • Renters who want lively, walkable streets with nightlife + restaurants
  • Buyers seeking charm-filled homes with Chicago architecture
  • Residents who want easy access to Lincoln Park, the lakefront, and nearby neighborhoods like River North or Lincoln Park

Homes & rentals in Old Town

Old Town offers a wide mix of housing, from vintage rentals and modern condos to renovated walk-ups and townhome communities.

What you’ll typically see

  • Vintage walk-up rentals with historic charm
  • Updated flats and condo conversions
  • Townhomes and low-rise buildings in quieter pockets
