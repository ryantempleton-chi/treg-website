Chicago neighborhood guide

streeterville.

Streeterville is a high-energy pocket along the lakefront, home to Navy Pier, premier hospitals, luxury high-rises, and some of the city’s most iconic architecture — all steps from the lake and Michigan Avenue.

Life in Streeterville

Streeterville blends lakefront living with downtown convenience — sleek high-rises, waterfront paths, cultural institutions, and walkable access to some of Chicago’s best amenities.

Lakefront access

Morning walks on the Lakefront Trail, quick beach time, and easy access to Navy Pier make this one of the most “water-adjacent” downtown neighborhoods.

High-rise living

Most housing is in full-amenity condo and rental towers — doormen, fitness centers, rooftop decks, and skyline/lake views are common here.

Hospitals & downtown convenience

Northwestern Medicine anchors the neighborhood, and Michigan Avenue + the Riverwalk are close enough to make errands, dining, and commuting feel effortless.
