Life in Streeterville

Streeterville offers a high-rise, lakefront lifestyle — with quick access to the Magnificent Mile, medical centers, the Riverwalk, and the lakefront trail.

Residents enjoy an extremely walkable environment, with grocery stores, restaurants, coffee shops, fitness studios, and everyday conveniences close by. The neighborhood feels urban and vertical, with many buildings offering lake, river, or skyline views.

Being near Northwestern Memorial Hospital and Northwestern’s downtown campus also makes Streeterville especially popular with medical and graduate professionals.

Who this area works well for

  • Professionals working downtown or in nearby medical/academic centers
  • Renters and buyers who want full-amenity high-rise living
  • Residents who value lake access, views, and walkability above all

Homes & rentals in Streeterville

Streeterville is primarily high-rise territory — with a strong mix of luxury rentals and condo buildings offering extensive amenities and a range of price points.

What you’ll typically see

  • Luxury rental towers with pools, gyms, and rooftop decks
  • Condo buildings with doormen, garages, and lake or city views
  • Some townhome-style and lower-rise options on select blocks
