Life in Streeterville

Streeterville combines city energy with lakefront calm — offering walkability, world-class amenities, and some of the best views in downtown Chicago.

The neighborhood is centered around the lakefront trail, the Chicago River, and the iconic stretch of Michigan Avenue. Residents love the convenience — everything from groceries to gyms to restaurants is within a short walk.

Streeterville’s buildings range from newer glass towers to established condos with spacious floor plans. Many offer full-service amenities, stunning views, and direct access to outdoor recreation.

Who this area works well for

  • Professionals who value walkability and lakefront access
  • Residents who want modern amenities in full-service buildings
  • Medical students & healthcare workers commuting to Northwestern

Homes & rentals in Streeterville

Streeterville is known for its high-rise living — with luxury rentals, condo towers, and a mix of modern and classic buildings offering something for nearly every lifestyle.

What you’ll typically see

  • Luxury rental towers with extensive amenity packages
  • Condo buildings with lake, river, or skyline views
  • Mid-rise buildings offering quieter, more traditional layouts
