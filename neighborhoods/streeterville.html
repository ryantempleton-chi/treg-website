streeterville.

Streeterville is a high-energy pocket along the lakefront, home to Navy Pier, premier hospitals, luxury high-rises, and some of the city’s most iconic architecture — all steps from the lake and Michigan Avenue.

Life in Streeterville

Streeterville blends lakefront living with downtown convenience — sleek high-rises, waterfront paths, cultural institutions, and walkable access to some of Chicago's best amenities.

Residents enjoy quick access to Northwestern Medicine, the MCA, the Riverwalk, and endless restaurants and shops along Michigan Avenue. With a dense mix of rental and condo towers, most homes offer skyline views, doorman buildings, and modern conveniences.

Whether you want waterfront access, proximity to work or school, or a luxury high-rise lifestyle, Streeterville offers some of the most convenient city living in Chicago.
