a structured process for a smoother sale

No two homes or sellers are the same — but a clear process creates consistency. We focus on preparation, presentation, and communication so you always know what’s next.

Below is the general framework we follow with sellers. We’ll tailor the pace and priorities to your home, your timeline, and what matters most to you.

what to expect, step by step

  1. Step 1: Consult + plan
    We align on goals, timing, and what a successful sale looks like — then complete a walkthrough and map the plan.
  2. Step 2: Pricing
    We review comps, competition, and buyer behavior to set a pricing + positioning strategy that fits your goals.
  3. Step 3: Prep
    We identify the highest-impact touch-ups, staging guidance, and presentation details that help buyers say “yes.”
  4. Step 4: Launch
    We coordinate photography, craft listing copy, and launch with clean marketing so your first impression is strong.
  5. Step 5: Showings + offers
    We manage showings, track feedback, and negotiate offers strategically — weighing price, terms, and buyer strength.
  6. Step 6: Contract to close
    We coordinate inspection, appraisal, attorneys, and deadlines — keeping everything organized through closing.

when to reach out

You don’t have to be “ready tomorrow” to start the conversation. The best outcomes often come from connecting early — so we can plan prep, pricing, and timing in a calm, intentional way.

Whether you’re a few weeks out or still exploring the idea, we’re happy to talk through what the process could look like for your home.

common selling questions

How early should I start planning to sell?
Ideally, 4–8 weeks before you want to list (sometimes longer) — so we can plan prep, photography, and timing without rushing.

Do I need to paint or renovate before listing?
Not always. We’ll focus on what moves the needle: presentation, clean repairs, and first impressions — without overspending.

How do you determine the right price?
We use comps, competition, buyer behavior, and your home’s positioning. Pricing is strategy — and we’ll explain the “why” behind it.

What happens if we don’t get strong activity right away?
We’ll track showings, feedback, and online engagement early. If the market is telling us something, we’ll adjust quickly and intentionally.

Do I have to leave during showings?
In most cases, yes. We’ll coordinate access and help you plan a showing strategy that’s realistic for your schedule.

How long does closing take after we accept an offer?
It depends on financing and timelines, but many Chicago closings fall in the 30–45 day range. We’ll coordinate deadlines and keep it moving.
