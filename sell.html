selling with a plan, not guesswork

A great sale doesn’t start with a sign in the yard or a listing going live — it starts with a clear strategy. We’ll look at your goals, your timing, and the story of your home, then build a plan that feels realistic and intentional.

Together, we’ll review recent comparable sales, local trends, and your home’s condition to arrive at a pricing and prep strategy that makes sense. From there, we’ll talk through what to fix, what to feature, and what can stay as-is — so you’re investing your time and money in the right places.

Once we go live, you’ll know what to expect: how showings will work, how feedback will be handled, and how we’ll adjust if the market speaks differently than the data did.

what you can expect as a seller

  • Honest pricing guidance based on data, condition, and timing.
  • Prep and staging recommendations that fit your budget and bandwidth.
  • Modern marketing with professional photos and thoughtful listing copy.
  • Structured showings with clear communication and feedback.
  • Offer and negotiation strategy tailored to your priorities.

chicago, neighborhoods, and beyond

Whether your property is in a classic Chicago neighborhood or just outside the city, we’ll help you position it thoughtfully within the broader market.

We’ll talk through how your home compares to nearby listings and recent sales — and how buyers are currently behaving in your price bracket. From there, we’ll decide together how to show up competitively while still honoring your goals for timeline and bottom line.

If you’re also buying a new home, we’ll help you coordinate both sides of the move so the process feels as smooth as possible.
