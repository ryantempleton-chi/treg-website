lombard.

A convenient western suburb with strong access, a solid downtown pocket, and a wide housing mix — a “best of both worlds” option for buyers who want value, connectivity, and everyday convenience.

Life in Lombard

Lombard is a practical, well-located choice: easy highways, Metra access, and a lot of “life infrastructure” — shopping, parks, restaurants, and quick access to nearby western suburbs. The community is a mix of established neighborhoods and newer pockets, making it a flexible option for a wide range of buyers.

Downtown & local spots

A walkable downtown pocket with restaurants, coffee, and casual “weeknight plans” energy — plus a lot of nearby options in surrounding suburbs.

Homes & variety

A broad mix: classic single-family neighborhoods, mid-century homes, some newer construction, plus condo and townhome options in convenient locations.

Access & commute

Strong access to I-88 / I-355 corridors plus Metra options — a good fit for city commuters and suburban-to-suburban routines.
