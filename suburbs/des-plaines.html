A Northwest suburb with a “best of both worlds” setup — Metra access, strong highway connectivity, and a wide mix of housing with easy reach to O’Hare and the city.

Life in Des Plaines

Des Plaines is a pragmatic pick for buyers who want flexibility: city access when you need it, plus quick routes to the Northwest suburbs and major employment corridors. The vibe is established and comfortable, with classic homes in many neighborhoods and pockets of newer development closer to key roads.

Downtown & daily life

A steady mix of everyday amenities — shopping, dining, and local routines that make it easy to live here long-term.

Homes & options

A broad mix: classic single-family homes, some townhome/condo inventory, and select newer pockets near key corridors.

Commute & access

Strong connectivity: Metra access plus major roads for commuters — a go-to for buyers who want flexibility across directions.
