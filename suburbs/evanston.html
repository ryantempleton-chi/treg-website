evanston.

A lakefront North Shore suburb with real city energy — walkable districts, CTA + Metra access, and a housing mix that ranges from historic homes to modern condos near the action.

Life in Evanston

Evanston is a favorite for buyers who want a “suburb” that doesn’t feel sleepy: coffee shops, restaurants, multiple commercial districts, and easy transit into Chicago. Add the lakefront, architecture, and a wide range of housing — and you get a place that feels uniquely its own, not just an extension of the city.

Walkable districts

Multiple “nodes” with their own vibe — from downtown Evanston to neighborhood corridors with strong local businesses.

Homes & variety

Historic homes and classic architecture, plus condo and townhome options — especially near transit and downtown pockets.

Transit & lakefront access

CTA + Metra access gives real flexibility, and the lakefront is a major lifestyle perk for runners, walkers, and beach days.
