libertyville.

A go-to North/Northwest suburb with a real downtown, strong community culture, and a “best of both worlds” feel — lively near the center, quieter and more residential as you head outward.

Life in Libertyville

Libertyville is popular for a reason: the downtown is active (restaurants, coffee, events), the community feels established, and you get a lot of everyday convenience — parks, trails, and easy access for commuting or suburban-to-suburban travel. It’s a strong fit for buyers who want “suburb living” without feeling disconnected from things to do.

Downtown energy

A true suburban downtown — dining, cafés, and community events that keep Libertyville feeling active beyond just weekends.

Homes & neighborhoods

A broad mix — classic single-family streets, some newer construction pockets, and options that range from “starter” to “move-up,” depending on location and lot size.

Parks & access

Trails and green space are a big perk, plus practical access to commuter routes for Chicago trips and suburban connectivity.
