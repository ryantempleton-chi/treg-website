arlington heights.

A top Northwest suburb with a strong downtown, Metra access, and a “best of both worlds” feel — walkable pockets and nightlife near the center, plus quiet residential streets and parks throughout.

Life in Arlington Heights

Arlington Heights is a go-to for buyers who want classic suburb living without feeling disconnected. The downtown area is legitimately active — restaurants, coffee, events, and easy Metra access — while the broader community is made up of established neighborhoods, family-friendly amenities, and everyday convenience for shopping, schools, and commuting.

Downtown & local spots

A true “go out close to home” suburb — a dense downtown strip with dining, cafés, entertainment, and community events that keep the area feeling lively year-round.

Homes & neighborhoods

A broad mix: mid-century ranches, larger two-stories, renovated classics, some newer construction, and condo options near downtown — with mature trees and a “settled-in” feel.

Commute & access

Metra access to downtown Chicago, plus strong road connectivity for suburban-to-suburban commutes. You’re also close to major retail corridors and nearby North/Northwest suburbs.
