About Ryan

Ryan Templeton is the Lead Broker & Founder of the Templeton Real Estate Group — a boutique Chicago team built on relationships, not transactions. With nearly a decade of experience helping clients buy, sell, and rent across the city, Ryan brings a calm, organized, and straightforward approach to every move.

From first-time renters planning their very first lease to buyers and sellers navigating their next big chapter, Ryan’s focus is on clarity, communication, and long-term trust. When he’s not working with clients, you’ll often find him exploring new neighborhoods, checking out Chicago’s restaurant scene, or taking in whatever the city has to offer — from seasonal events to game days and everything in between.
